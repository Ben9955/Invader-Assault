Enemies & Objects

Discover the threats and treasures of deep space. Each enemy and object brings unique challenges or advantages to your mission.

Big Boss

The Eyebeam Overlord is your toughest challenge. It shoots powerful beams from its eyes and is incredibly hard to defeat. Only the best can bring this alien down.

Blob Alien

Slow but deadly, the Blob Alien explodes on contact with your ship, causing severe damage. It can’t shoot, but you’ll need to destroy it before it gets too close.

Gun Alienr

Armed with a plasma blaster, the Gun Alien fires rapid projectiles from a distance. It’s fast and dangerous—take it down before it overwhelms you with firepower.

Beetlemorph

Small and numerous, Beetlemorphs can’t shoot but deal minor damage if touched. They’re harmless if they reach the bottom of the screen, so choose your battles wisely

Asteroid

Destroying an asteroid earns points, and sometimes a power-up will appear in its place. If it collides with the ship, it deals small damage.

Unlockable Spaceships

Choose your ship wisely. Each spaceship has unique abilities, and some require a high level of experience points (XP) to unlock. Save up or spend now—the choice is yours.

Bomber

Your trusty starter ship, Bomber is reliable and balanced. No XP is needed to use this ship, making it the perfect choice for rookies and veterans alike.

Corvette

A tactical ship with advanced shields and firepower. Requires 500 XP to unlock. Corvette is ideal for players looking to withstand heavy enemy fire.

Fighter

The ultimate ship, Fighter has unmatched speed and weaponry. Only true space heroes can unlock this ship with 1000 XP. Save up or wait—Fighter is worth it.

Power-Ups

Discover essential power-ups to enhance your spaceship's abilities. Each one offers unique advantages to help you survive the dangers of deep space.

Health Boost

Restores a portion of your spaceship’s health, giving you the resilience needed to face tougher enemies. Grab it when you're running low on health.

Full-System Upgrade

Temporarily enhances your spaceship’s overall performance, boosting weapon power, firing rate, and even speed. Each upgrade collected strengthens your ship, making you a formidable force in space.

XP Bonus

Earn extra experience points, helping you unlock advanced ships and upgrades faster. Every XP bonus brings you closer to unlocking powerful ships.
